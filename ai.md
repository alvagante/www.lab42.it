## Exploring the Frontier

Artificial intelligence is reshaping how software is built, how knowledge is accessed,
and how humans and machines collaborate. Lab42's AI unit sits at the intersection
of practical engineering and forward-looking research.

We build tools, explore new paradigms, and help organizations understand and apply
AI — with a focus on developer tooling, automation, and augmented intelligence.
