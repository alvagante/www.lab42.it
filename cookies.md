## What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve performance, and provide information to site owners.

---

## Cookies on this site

This site is a static website with **no analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies**.

### Technically necessary

This site does not set any first-party cookies.

### Third-party connections

When you visit this site, your browser may connect to third-party services that could set cookies or store data independently of this site:

| Service | Purpose | Provider's policy |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| Google Fonts | Loading web fonts (Inter, JetBrains Mono) | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| GitHub Pages | Site hosting infrastructure | [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) |

Google Fonts requests may cause your IP address to be sent to Google's servers. Since May 2022, Google Fonts can be configured to not log IP addresses — our implementation uses the standard CDN embed. If you prefer to avoid this connection, you can block `fonts.googleapis.com` and `fonts.gstatic.com` in your browser or via a DNS-level blocker; the site will fall back to system fonts.

## Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Blocking cookies from third-party services may affect the visual rendering of this site (fonts), but will not affect access to any content.
