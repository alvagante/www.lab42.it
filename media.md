## Stories Worth Telling

Technology is made by people — and people are far more interesting than any stack
or methodology. Lab42 Media produces podcasts that go beyond the hype, exploring
the human, cultural, and often chaotic side of working in tech.

We talk with practitioners, thinkers, and occasional misfits about what it's really
like to build things, break things, and learn in this industry.

## Why we make podcasts

<div class="two-col">
  <div>
    <h2>Knowledge should be shared</h2>
    <p>
      Too much hard-won expertise lives in Slack threads and post-mortem docs that
      nobody reads. Podcasts let practitioners share real experience — the kind of
      knowledge you can't find in official documentation.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <h2>The community matters</h2>
    <p>
      Both shows grew out of Lab42's involvement in the open-source and DevOps
      community. They're a way to give back, spark conversations, and keep a
      human thread running through an industry that can get very abstract.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
